About Vizag

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a beautiful port city on the southeast coast of India. It is known for its scenic beaches, lush green hills, and a vibrant cultural heritage. With its modern infrastructure and natural beauty, Vizag is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat by the beach or an adventure in the hills, Vizag has something for everyone. Explore its rich history, indulge in delicious cuisine, and experience the warmth of its people.
